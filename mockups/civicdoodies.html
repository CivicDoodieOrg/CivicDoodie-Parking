Paid for parking and still got a ticket?

If the ParkMobile zone on the curb sign doesn't match the zone in the app, you shouldn't be penalized. Check a zone in seconds — and put it on the map.


How it works

  1. 1
    Photograph the sign. Snap the ParkMobile zone number printed on the curb.
  2. 2
    Confirm both zones. We read the sign's number; you enter the one your app showed.
  3. 3
    It goes on the map. If they mismatch, the zone is logged — and every repeat report adds to its count.

What we collect

When you report a zone, CivicDoodies records the zone numbers, an approximate location, any photo you attach, and a name only if you choose to enter one. No account is required.

Location and name

Location is used to place your report on the map. The name field is optional — leave it blank and the report stays anonymous.
